Dropped tables in recycle bin

Use 'PURGE RECYCLEBIN' to free space of dropped tables from recycle bin
SELECT ROUND(r.Space * t.Block_Size / (1024*1024), 3) Size_MB, r.*
                    FROM   DBA_RecycleBin r
                    LEFT OUTER JOIN DBA_Tablespaces t ON t.TableSpace_Name = r.TS_Name
                    ORDER BY Size_MB DESC NULLS LAST
